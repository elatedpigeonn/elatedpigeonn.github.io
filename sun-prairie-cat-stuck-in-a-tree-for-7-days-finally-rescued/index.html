Sun Prairie cat stuck in a tree for 7 days finally rescued

adminOctober 20, 2019News, Top Stories

SUN PRAIRIE (WKOW) — Tommy the cat has finally made it back home after spending almost a week in a tree, thanks to help from a generous community.

Last Monday, neighbors tell 27 News they spotted Tommy in a large tree at Birkibine Park in Sun Prairie.

Several attempts to get him down by a group of neighbors were unsuccessful.

According to a neighbor, Sun Prairie Fire Department also attempted to reach him.

These attempts to get him down only resulted in Tommy going further up the tree.

Sunday morning, with help from some neighbors, Tommy’s owner Sue was finally located.

Sue confirmed that Tommy was her cat and he had been missing for almost a week.

With the use of a neighbor’s ladder, Sue was able to get up into the lower part of the tree and call his name.

Tommy recognized her voice also immediately.

He climbed down close enough for Sue to grab him and get him out of the tree.

Tommy is now back home with his family.

The news of the cat’s predicament spread on a social media group for the Sun Prairie community.

The conversations and comments sparked concerns and generated ideas to try to get Tommy down from the tree, before it was clear who was the owner.
